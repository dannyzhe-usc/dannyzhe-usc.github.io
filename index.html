What We Do

Northstar Engineering Intelligence provides advanced engineering data analysis, artificial intelligence, and machine learning solutions to support problem solving, root cause analysis, and decision-making in engineering and industrial applications. We specialize in integrating multi-modal data sources—including testing, measurement, inspection, manufacturing, and field data—to deliver actionable insights, probabilistic analysis, and optimized solutions. Our engineering-first AI systems combine physics knowledge with probabilistic machine learning to produce fast, explainable, and traceable results—not black-box predictions.
